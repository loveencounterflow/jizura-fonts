- About GL-Tsukiji-5go font

  GL-Tsukiji is the HIRAGANA and KATAKANA fonts from typeface of the Tokyo Tsukiji Kappan, Japanese type
  foundry in Meiji period (1868-1912).

  GL-Tsukiji-5go supports... U+0020, U+3000, U+3005, U+303B, U+3041-U+3096, U+3099-U+309E, U+30A1-U+30FF,
  U+31F0-U+31FF,  and some Chinese Letters below.
